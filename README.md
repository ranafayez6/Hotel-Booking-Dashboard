
# Hotel Booking Analysis – Project Summary

# Project Overview

This project analyzes hotel booking data using Power BI to uncover trends in revenue, customer behavior, booking patterns, and cancellation rates.
The dashboard was designed to help hotels make data-driven decisions that improve operational performance and customer experience.

⸻

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze hotel booking performance over time.
* Identify factors affecting booking cancellations.
* Understand customer behavior and booking trends.
* Track revenue performance and occupancy insights.
* Discover the most profitable customer segments and booking channels.

⸻

# 🔍 Key Insights

* Summer season generated the highest revenue and booking volume.
* Online Travel Agencies (OTA) contributed the largest share of bookings.
* Higher lead time bookings showed higher cancellation probability.
* Repeat guests had lower cancellation behavior compared to new guests.
* Couples and families generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Non-refundable deposits significantly reduced cancellation rates.
* Certain market segments showed consistently high cancellation levels.

⸻

# 💡 Recommendations

* Apply stricter cancellation policies for high-risk bookings.
* Increase marketing campaigns during high-performing seasons.
* Encourage direct bookings to reduce OTA dependency.
* Offer loyalty programs for repeat guests.
* Optimize pricing strategies based on seasonality and lead time.
* Focus on customer segments with higher revenue potential.
